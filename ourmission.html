Our mission

At Pawsome Adoption, our unwavering commitment is to make the world a better place for our furry companions. Our mission and vision are the guiding principles that drive us to create a haven for dogs in need, ensuring their happiness and well-being while uniting them with loving families.

Our mission is simple yet profound to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need. We understand that every dog deserves a chance at a joyful life, regardless of their background. At Pawsome Adoption, we strive to provide a second chance to these loyal companions, offering them a safe and loving environment where they can thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where every dog finds a forever home filled with love, care, and happiness. We envision a community that values the unconditional love and companionship that dogs offer, and where responsible pet ownership is championed.

Pawsome Adoption is committed to setting the standard for dog adoption centers, not only in providing top-notch care for our four-legged residents but also in educating the public about the responsibilities and joys of pet ownership. We strive to be a beacon of hope, setting an example of excellence in animal welfare.

Thank you for helping dogs in need

When donating, you can make a remarkable difference in the lives of the dogs under our care at Pawsome Adoption. With your generous contribution, we can provide vital support in various ways:

Food and Nutrition

A donation of 100 SEK allows us to purchase nutritious meals to feed a hungry dog for an entire month. Proper nutrition is the cornerstone of their well-being, and your support ensures they have healthy and balanced meals.

Veterinary Costs

Your contribution can also cover essential veterinary costs such as vaccinations, deworming, and routine check-ups. This ensures that each animal receives the necessary medical attention to stay healthy and happy.

Adoption costs

Your generous gift can support our efforts to find loving forever homes for our rescued animals. It covers the costs associated with adoption events and thorough screening processes to ensure a perfect match.


Every penny brings us one step closer to achieving our mission of providing love, care, and a brighter future for animals in need. Your support is invaluable and greatly appreciated.

Together, we can make a significant impact on the lives of these wonderful creatures.
Thank you for your compassion and generosity!
